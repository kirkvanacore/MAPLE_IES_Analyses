Fidelity Metrics Analysis

This markdown contains analyses of the cross-program/condition fidelity metrics listed below. The analyses include visualizations and statistical tests of the fidelity by program/condition as well as analyses of how these metrics vary by nesting units (school, teacher, class)
Key Variables:

  • Number of Assignments Started (fidelity_started_sum)
  • Number of Assignments Complete (fidelity_complete_sum)

Takeways:

  • Students in the Dragon Box condition had signficantly lower assignments started and complete.
  • Both metrics had bimodal distributions, suggesting two distnict populations: high and low fedility
  • Much of the vairance in fedility was assocaited with students nexting units (school and class). Intra-class correlations for both school/class ranged from .29 to .36
